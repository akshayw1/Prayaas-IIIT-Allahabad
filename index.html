Meal Distribution

On 30th April, Team Prayaas welcomed the students of Prayaas with an evening meal for over 200+ children. We also distributed stationery items like pens and writing supplies. This was an overwhelming day for us which would not have been possible without the immense support of our faculties and the students involved.
